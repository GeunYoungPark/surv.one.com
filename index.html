About our company

A successful building project starts with a precise Land Survey. SURVCORP Pty Ltd is a multi-faceted, full service Land Surveying company with consulting services available as needed for all types of projects. We have provided quality of services and technical excellence throughtout NSW
Director Simon Ho, a Registered Surveyor in NSW has over 20 years of local experience and all staffs are locally trained and qualified. Our staff of professional Land Surveryor, Cad Technician Specialist and Management team are able to provide an array of surveying services for construction and building industries, and land developers
